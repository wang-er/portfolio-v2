About Me.

I'm Erin Wang, a Computer Science and Design student at Northeastern University. Over here, I'm able to take classes on two subjects that I enjoy doing: art and code!

Throughout my life I've been fascinated by how computers worked and how technology has been rapidly developing throughout my lifetime. Through technology, so many fields have grown to become more advanced. However, as a child, I quickly grew attached to the art side of technology. I loved playing around with Adobe Photoshop and place random blocks on code on Scratch to create simple games.

Over time, I've come to realize that I want to not only enjoy my life, I want to help others. I hope that in the future, I can create designs that can be clear and pleasing to the general public.

Other than that, I have a huge passion for board games. A few of my favorites includes Terraforming Mars, Mansions of Maddness, and Arboretum!
