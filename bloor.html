Buidling a Better Bloor Dufferin

An AR Experience for a call to action.

Buidling a Better Bloor Dufferin is an AR Experience done with a Mobile App called Hoverlay. For this project, we were able to meet up with locals from Toronto, Canada. Over there, they requested us to make a prototype of an AR Experience for their cause. During the recent times, like any other big city, Toronto as been keen on developing bigger and more expensive buildings. However, these buildings like expensive apartments and malls cause local businesses to close down, and forces those who can't afford the growing cost of living to move out. These developments are happening all over Toronto, and local neighborhoods are trying their hardest to avoid the dangerous modernization. One particular neighborhood, Bloor-Dufferin, requested us students to make a short AR experience regarding the potential changes, and how these changes can have a huge problem in the future.

There's so much more I could say about this project, but I think the work document is able to describe our whole process documentation.
